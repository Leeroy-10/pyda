ABOUT PENDLE YOUTH DARTS ACADEMY



Pendle Youth Darts Academy is a non-profitable organisation launched in October 2024 by the founders Gary Hooper and Mark Edwards along with the support of our amazing coaches, volunteers and sponsors.

The academy aims to promote the Sport of Darts to children and young people who are the future of the sport. The academy enrol both boys and girls from the ages of 8 to 17 years old. We have a three tier training system based around their experience - the training categories are Novices, Intermediates and Elites. We also hold weekly coaching sessions and have various competitions throughout the year.

We currently have 35 students on our books with a waiting list of over 20 due to the ever-growing interest in the sport. All funds collected go back into the academy by paying for equipment, competitions, trophies, trips and general running costs.



WHERE AND WHEN



Venue - David Street Club, Barrowford, Lancashire, BB9 6HL

When - Saturday Mornings - 10:30am - 1:00pm

Who - Ages 8 to 17 years old for both boys and girls

Cost - £6 per session

We are fully insured with public liability insurance, and all coaches and volunteers are DBS checked.
